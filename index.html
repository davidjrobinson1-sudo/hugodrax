Activate & pay

Set up your wearable in minutes.

Manage-Mii curve
01

Account setup & linking your wearable

Download: Get the Manage-Mii app on your smartphone via the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.

Registration: Open the app and register with your phone number or email. You will receive an SMS or email to confirm your registration.

Pairing: In the app, select "Add wearable." Hold your device against the NFC reader on the back of your phone until it connects. You may need to remove your phone case if it interferes with the signal.

02

Adding payment services

Once your wearable is paired, you can link it to your personal credentials and attach a contactless service. You can add a virtual payment method or credit card, such as Curve, depending on your region and the compatible brands you use.

Need wider card support?

If your bank card is not directly supported, Curve will help you connect more of your everyday cards to RINGO. Create a Curve digital card, link eligible Mastercard, Visa, or UK PayPal accounts, then connect Curve to your ring.

Available in the UK and EEA.

03

Managing security

Freeze operation: If you lose your wearable or it gets stolen, you can instantly freeze its functionality directly within the app.

Track activity: Monitor transaction history and service activity through your user account dashboard.
