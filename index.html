How it works

Secure payments, built into a ring.

RINGO uses passive NFC technology to communicate with compatible contactless readers. Your payment credentials are protected through tokenization, so the ring can be convenient without exposing your actual card details at the terminal.

FAQ

Questions people ask first.

Does RINGO need charging?

No. The ring uses passive NFC, so it does not need a battery or charging cable.

Is it waterproof?

The ceramic shell is designed for everyday wear, including hand washing and active use.

How do payments stay secure?

Payment rings use tokenized credentials, meaning the terminal does not receive your raw card details.

Can I use it everywhere?

RINGO is intended for compatible contactless payment terminals. Availability depends on card setup, issuer support, and region.
